# XR-13.0.1 — Visible Mission Contract Hotfix

## Decision

XR-13 user-friendly recomposition correctly tries to remove duplicate guidance, but it hid the `welcomeCard` element. That broke the RTL/mobile browser contract and also removed a known visible anchor used by the first-flow smoke path.

This hotfix keeps the `welcomeCard` browser-visible while reducing it to a compact mission strip. The goal is not to restore the old repeated guidance block. The goal is to keep the user-friendly structure and preserve the required contract.

## What changed

- Keep `welcomeCard` visible.
- Convert the card into a compact mission strip.
- Keep repeated body copy, checklist, and support text visually demoted.
- Keep duplicate guidance collapsed elsewhere.
- Keep `stage bar` markup list-labelled with the expected contiguous token.
- Preserve runtime IDs.
- Preserve RTL/mobile behavior.
- Preserve export contracts.
- Preserve the XR-8 evidence matrix.

## UX classification

The mission card is now treated as a SYSTEM + HELP anchor:

- SYSTEM because existing tests require `.welcomeCard` to remain browser-visible.
- HELP because it gives a compact first-action cue without repeating the full onboarding explanation.

## Acceptance criteria

- `.welcomeCard` is browser-visible in Arabic RTL/mobile.
- The first-flow remains user-friendly and does not restore the old vertical guidance dump.
- The stage bar keeps the `id="stageBar" role="list"` contract.
- Duplicate guidance remains collapsed.
- Runtime IDs remain unchanged.
- XR-8 evidence matrix remains unchanged.

## Next

After this hotfix passes, XR-13 can be committed together with this document, then the regenerated screenshots should be audited for actual user clarity rather than just visual cleanliness.
